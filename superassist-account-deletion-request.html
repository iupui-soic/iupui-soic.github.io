SuperAssist - Account deletion request

To request deletion of your SuperAssist account and associated data:

  1. Email your request to saptpurk{at}iu.edu
  2. Include your registered email address
  3. Use subject line "SuperAssist Account Deletion Request"

Upon receiving your request:

  • Your account will be deleted within 7 days
  • All personal data will be permanently removed
  • Anonymous usage statistics may be retained
  • Supervision records will be archived according to healthcare retention requirements

Contact us at saptpurk{at}iu.edu if you have any questions about the deletion process.
